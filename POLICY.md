# CivicConnect Policies

## 1. Data Privacy Policy (DPDP Act 2023 Aligned)
CivicConnect is committed to protecting citizen data.
- **Consent:** Users explicitly consent to share location and issue details for the purpose of resolution.
- **Anonymisation:** Citizens can opt for "Anonymous Submission". In this mode, no personally identifiable information (PII) is stored or displayed publicly.
- **Purpose Limitation:** Data is used strictly for municipal issue resolution and performance tracking.
- **Right to Erasure:** Users can request the removal of their reports and associated data.

## 2. Escalation Policy (SLA)
To ensure timely resolution, the following Service Level Agreements (SLAs) are enforced:

| Issue Category   | Resolution Time (SLA) | Escalation Trigger |
|------------------|-----------------------|--------------------|
| Public Safety    | 12 Hours              | Instant (L1 to L2) |
| Sanitation       | 24 Hours              | 24h + 1m           |
| Encroachment     | 48 Hours              | 48h + 1m           |
| Infrastructure   | 72 Hours              | 72h + 1m           |

### Escalation Workflow:
1. **Level 1 (Ward Officer):** Initial assignment upon report submission.
2. **Level 2 (Assistant Commissioner):** Automatic escalation if SLA is breached.
3. **Public Flagging:** Issues past 2x SLA time are highlighted on the Public Dashboard to nudge resolution.

## 3. Anti-Harassment & Moderation
- **Duplicate Detection:** System flags reports within 10 meters with similar categories as "Potential Duplicate".
- **Moderation:** Admin portal allows flagging malicious or harassing content. Verified harassment leads to IP-level reporting to authorities.
